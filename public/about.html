About

I specialise in hand-painted botanical illustrations using gouache, inspired by the art and science of the natural world. My work balances delicate detail with vibrant colour, capturing the character of leaves, stems, and blooms in a way that feels both timeless and fresh.

My aim is simple: to bring a sense of calm, wonder and connection to nature through every brushstroke. Whether you're here to explore my artwork, collaborate on a project, or find the perfect piece for your space, I'm glad you've found your way here.
